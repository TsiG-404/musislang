Artist Information

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye (Amharic: አቤል መኮንን ተስፋዬ; né Abel Tesfaye, born February 16, 1990), known professionally as the Weeknd, is a Canadian singer-songwriter, record producer, and actor.[2][3] He is best known for his light-lyric tenor vocal range and falsetto, as well as his signature alternative R&B sound. His accolades include four Grammy Awards, 20 Billboard Music Awards, 22 Juno Awards, six American Music Awards, three MTV Video Music Awards, and a Latin Grammy Award.
